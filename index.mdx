## What is PROJEIC?

PROJEIC (Project Engineering Integration Center) is a project management platform built for the EIC engineering school. It supports two categories of projects:

- **Institutional projects** — tied to university subjects (ramos), with professors as supervisors and students as team members
- **Internal projects** — free-form engineering initiatives not linked to a curriculum

The backend handles all data persistence, authentication, file uploads, notifications, and analytics through a unified GraphQL API.

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Authenticate with Google">
    Redirect your users to `/projeic/api/auth/google` to start the OAuth flow. After authorization, you receive a JWT token.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send your first query">
    Include the JWT token as a `Bearer` token in the `Authorization` header and query the GraphQL endpoint at `/projeic/api/graphql`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a project">
    Use the `createProject` mutation to create your first project, then add members and tasks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore the full API">
    Browse the [API Reference](/api/overview) to discover all available queries and mutations.
  </Step>
</Steps>
